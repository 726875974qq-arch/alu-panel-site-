FAQ

Questions Buyers Ask Us Most

What is your MOQ (Minimum Order Quantity)?

We accept orders from 100 m² for standard panels. Custom carved / perforated designs typically start at 200 m².

How long does production take?

Standard PVDF solid panels: 15–25 days after deposit. Complex carved or double-curved panels: 30–45 days. Rush orders can be arranged.

What payment terms do you accept?

30% T/T deposit + 70% before shipment is standard. We also work with Letter of Credit at sight for orders over USD 50,000.

Do you provide installation?

We don't send installation crews abroad, but we provide full shop drawings, training videos, and video-call support for your local installers.

Can you match a specific color?

Yes — send us any RAL / Pantone number, or mail us a physical swatch. Color match within ΔE<1, confirmed with a physical sample before production.

What's included in the warranty?

Up to 25 years on PVDF color retention (≤5 ΔE), up to 15 years on film integrity, plus 2 years on manufacturing defects. Written warranty with every shipment.

Can I visit your factory?

Absolutely. Email your travel dates and we'll arrange airport pickup from Guangzhou (CAN), Shenzhen (SZX) or Hong Kong (HKG).
