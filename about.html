Our Story

From one coating line in 2005 to a global facade brand.

China Custom Aluminium Panel Supplier was founded in Foshan — China's aluminum heartland — with a single mission: make aluminum curtain wall panels good enough to compete with European suppliers, and sell them at a Chinese price.

Two decades later, we operate an 80,000 m² factory with four production lines, supply contractors and developers in over 60 countries, and count 1,500+ completed projects in our reference book. Along the way, we've learned that international buyers care about three things above all: quality you can verify, delivery you can plan around, and communication you don't have to chase.

That's what we optimize for — every day, every order.

Our Factory

Full In-House Production

Every step from raw coil to finished panel happens under one roof — no subcontractors, no surprises.

Cutting & Shearing

CNC shears for precise blanking of aluminum coils up to 2.5 m wide.

Punching & Carving

6 CNC routers, 4 turret punches, 2 fiber lasers for perforated and carved patterns.

Bending & Forming

Press brakes up to 6 m, roll-formers for curved panels, skilled team for double-curvature.

Welding & Riveting

Argon-arc welding, hidden riveting, structural gluing for premium finishes.

Cleaning & Pre-treatment

7-stage chromate-free pre-treatment — the unsung hero of long-lasting PVDF.

PVDF Coating

Two coating lines running Valspar / PPG 70% PVDF, 3-coat + topcoat.

Quality Inspection

Gloss meter, coating thickness gauge, salt-spray chamber, adhesion test — per panel.

Packing & Loading

Fumigated export crates, foam layering, corner protection. Shipped globally.
